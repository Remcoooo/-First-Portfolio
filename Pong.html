My first game: Pong

Pong is the first game i've ever made.
This project was made with Unity in my first period of the education Sofware Developement.
Here i learned the beginnings of coding on how to create a game.
We mostly used C# as coding language and some build in features of Unity itself.
I stared with making the basic stuff, make a ball and let it have gravity and collision.
After i made this work i started working on the paddles and let them move
using the arrow keys or w and s you can go up or down.
I also made a score system that autmatically adds points to the player who scores.
After i finished the game itself and giving it a better look, i worked on the finishing touches
